Selected Ambient Works 85-92
by Aphex Twin

Greatest for a debut album
Score: 97/100

review
Selected Ambient Works 85-92 is a damn good album, a staple of Ambient Techno, and the starting point of a new genre called Electronic Listening Music (read #1 of the notes). Although, not his first release using the Aphex Twin name, it is the first however to gain tons of attention from fans of Electronica and music enthusiast spaces. An atmospheric and innovative record at the time, it jumpstarted his career from playing raves at the plains of Cornwall to being one of the most respected and greatest Electronic artists in the modern day.

Into the substance of this record comes "Xtal", a beautiful ambient and chillout track, gorgeous pads on top of glistening synths with a four on four house beat makes this one of his most loved coming from his huge and vast discography. The second track, the 9 minute "Tha", almost has the same merit as Xtal with the lush airy synths and ambiance, but in addition with some blow-like drums and a bass line, the setting of this track feels as you're waiting for the metro from the voices and conversations happening in the back while you just jam with no worry in your mind. Just simple and good Chillout at the end.
